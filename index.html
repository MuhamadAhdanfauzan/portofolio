Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of web development services including front-end development, responsive website design, UI/UX design, and landing page creation. I specialize in building modern, user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.

What is your typical project timeline?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A simple landing page typically takes 3-5 days, while a full e-commerce website can take 2-4 weeks. I always provide a timeline estimate after discussing your project requirements.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, I typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions in my project packages to ensure the final result meets your expectations. Additional revisions can be discussed based on project needs.

What technologies do you use?

I work with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, Node.js, and various CSS frameworks. I stay updated with the latest tools and best practices to deliver high-quality solutions.
